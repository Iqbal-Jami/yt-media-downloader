## üõ°Ô∏è RECOMMENDATIONS FOR LEGAL PROTECTION

1. **Display Legal Notice Prominently**
   - Show disclaimer before first use
   - Require user agreement checkbox
   - Display permanently in footer

2. **Implement Usage Restrictions**
   - Rate limiting (10 downloads/hour)
   - Require user confirmation they own rights
   - Block commercial use cases

3. **Register LLC or Similar Entity**
   - Separate personal assets from business
   - Consult business attorney
   - Register in business-friendly jurisdiction

4. **Use Privacy-Protected Domain Registration**
   - Hide personal information from WHOIS
   - Available through Namecheap, Cloudflare

5. **Consider Offshore Hosting**
   - If pursuing commercially at scale
   - Countries with relaxed copyright enforcement
   - Consult international attorney

6. **Terms of Service Agreement**
   - Require explicit acceptance before use
   - Log acceptance with timestamp and IP
   - Make easily accessible

7. **Professional Legal Consultation**
   - Essential if going commercial
   - Intellectual property attorney
   - Budget $500-2000 for initial consultation
